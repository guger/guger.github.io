Principles

  • Privacy. It is a major concern of mine, to keep away from your personal data. Many applications claim for permissions they obviously don't need. This is exactly where my apps re-imagine software. Every piece of data getting collected and every permission requested is necessary for my app to run. If it's an additional feature, the request will be optional.
    My main focus is on how to build intelligent features, without uploading any data into the cloud. However, this is not always possible. If you're interested in which data is collected, and why, please have a look at my privacy notes.

    Privacy Policy - Notes
  • Open Source. Sincerity creates confidence. Open sourcing apps is the best way to accomplish a basis of trust. Together, we are able to create something new. Re-imagine ideas.
    Feel free to have a look on my code, submit issues, bring-up new ideas or contribute to my projects.
